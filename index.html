FAQ

Do I need a Binance account?

Which USDT network do you support?

For Trust Wallet and similar wallets, we support USDT TRC20. Always confirm the network before sending.

Can I use NatCash / MonCash without USDT?

Yes. NatCash/MonCash can be used for non-USDT money transfer services as well.

How long does it take?

Timing depends on method, amount, and network/bank processing. We confirm the expected processing time before you send.
